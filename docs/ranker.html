State Metric Ranker

Rank states by a metric for a given year

Metric:

Notes
  • Margin: difference between Democratic and Republican vote share (D − R). Displayed as e.g. D+5.2 to mean the Democratic candidate is ahead by 5.2 percentage points.
  • Two-party: excludes third-party votes and recalculates margins on a D vs R two-party basis.
  • Relative: the state's margin minus the national margin (how much more D or R the state is compared to the nation).
  • Delta: change in the chosen metric since the previous presidential election.
